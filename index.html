DhartiDhan Vermicompost  - Organic Fertilizer

DhartiDhan Organic vermicompost is a superior-quality fertilizer produced by earthworms processing aged cow dung into a nutrient-rich, chemical-free powerhouse. It fundamentally restores the biological life of your soil, passing deep nutrition directly into the roots of your crops to create stronger, disease-free plants and healthier harvests.

Designed for modern agriculture, horticulture, landscaping, nurseries, and home gardening, our vermicompost provides a sustainable and eco-friendly solution for improving soil fertility without the use of harmful chemicals.

100% Organic: Completely chemical-free and environmentally friendly.
Lab Tested: Certified 1985 Fertilizer Control Order (FCO) compliant, evaluated strictly for quality, consistency, and structural reliability.
NPK Rich: Natural source of Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium.
Deep Hydration: Maximizes soil water retention and reduces irrigation needs.

DhartiDhan Organics Premium Cocopeat
DhartiDhan Organics Premium Cocopeat

DhartiDhan Cocopeat is a high-quality growing medium manufactured from 100% natural coconut husk fibers. Processed under strict quality standards, our premium low EC (Electrical Conductivity). It acts as a lightweight, sponge-like powerhouse that holds onto moisture evenly while opening up vital air pockets so roots can breathe deeply without rotting. 
It serves as an eco-friendly, sustainable alternative to traditional soil and peat moss, helping you achieve faster seed germination, stronger plant development, and significantly reduced watering needs.

Low EC & Balanced pH: Pre-washed to ensure low salt content, preventing root burn and optimizing nutrient absorption.
Premium & Lab Tested: Evaluated strictly for quality, structural reliability, and consistency across every batch.
Water & Nutrient Saver: Retains moisture for longer periods, reducing irrigation frequency and nutrient runoff.
Root & Soil Aerator: Enhances root growth through optimal aeration while reducing soil compaction.
100% Organic & Renewable: Eco-friendly, chemical-free growing medium perfect for sustainable agriculture.

🌍 Protecting Earth & Humans: The market is full of deceptive products where chemicals are mixed into raw dung to fake the texture of vermicompost / dung manure / worm compost. These synthetic additives harm the earth from the moment they are made until they slowly dissolve. Our initiative goes directly against this. We deal only in pure, slow-made organic vermicompost to bring true, uncorrupted health back to your food and our planet.
