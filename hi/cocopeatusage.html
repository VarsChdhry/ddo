Applications 

​✔️Agriculture and Field Crops
✔️Mix with soil to improve moisture retention and soil structure.
✔️Nurseries and Seedlings
✔️Seed germination trays, nursery beds, and young plant propagation.
✔️Greenhouses and Polyhouses
✔️For growing vegetables, flowers, and high-value crops.
✔️Hydroponics
✔️Standalone substrate or in combination with perlite and other growing media.
✔️Home Gardens and Landscaping
✔️Perfect for potted plants, kitchen gardens, lawns, and ornamental plants.

​For Potting Mixes:
Mix cocopeat with soil, compost, or vermicompost in the desired ratio to improve moisture retention and aeration.

For Seed Germination:
Use hydrated cocopeat as a germination medium for faster and healthier seedling growth.

For Hydroponics:
Hydrate and rinse before use according to crop requirements.

For Agriculture:
Incorporate cocopeat into the soil around the root zone to improve water retention and soil health.
